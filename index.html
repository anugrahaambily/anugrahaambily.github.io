Science illustrator

Can you try recollecting the pages of your science textbook at school ?!
Do you remember skipping those text-filled black and white pages with no figures? When full-bodied pages make the text monotonous and tedious to understand, a self-explanatory figure with no legend kills the curiosity and creative thinking in readers. An effective illustration is one that can prompt the readers to connect with the supporting text through logical thinking.
Coloring up the typical school curriculum textbooks with such rational graphics can not just make it interesting, but can imprint the concepts well in the growing brains. Interacting with students and identifying what they require and then designing it through their thoughts is what I look up to, and has set my goal towards that end.
